Query: Mak a pie chrt of custmer count by segmnt in Germny.
Response:
- **RESOLVABLE:** yes
- **MESSAGE:** Here's a pie chart showing the distribution of customer segments in Germany.  The chart displays the proportion of customers belonging to each segment within the German market. This helps visualize the relative size of each customer segment in Germany.
- **SQL:** ```sql
WITH GermanCustomers AS (
  SELECT
    c.segment
  FROM dim_customer AS c
  JOIN dim_country AS co
    ON c.country_id = co.country_id
  WHERE
    co.country_name = 'Germany'
)
SELECT
  segment,
  COUNT(*) AS customer_count
FROM GermanCustomers
GROUP BY
  segment;
```
- **RESULT_TYPE:** chart
- **CHART_TYPE:** pie
- **INSIGHTS:**
  - The pie chart will clearly show the dominant customer segment in Germany.
  - It will highlight the relative proportions of each segment, revealing potential market share imbalances.
  - This visualization aids in understanding the customer base composition in Germany for targeted marketing or product development.
