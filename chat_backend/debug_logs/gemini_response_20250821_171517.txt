Query: Show a bar chrt of convrsions for Instagrm campains by campain nam in 2024.
Response:
RESOLVABLE: yes
MESSAGE: Here's a bar chart showing the number of conversions for each Instagram campaign in 2024.  The data is pulled from our marketing campaign records.  This visualization helps understand the relative success of different campaigns on Instagram.
SQL: ```sql
WITH InstagramCampaigns AS (
  SELECT
    c.campaign_name,
    m.conversions
  FROM dim_campaign AS c
  JOIN fact_marketing AS m
    ON c.campaign_id = m.campaign_id
  JOIN dim_date AS d
    ON m.date_id = d.date_id
  WHERE
    c.platform = 'Instagram' AND d.year = 2024
)
SELECT
  campaign_name,
  SUM(conversions) AS total_conversions
FROM InstagramCampaigns
GROUP BY
  campaign_name
ORDER BY
  total_conversions DESC;
```
RESULT_TYPE: chart
CHART_TYPE: bar
INSIGHTS:
- The chart clearly shows which Instagram campaigns in 2024 generated the most conversions.
- We can easily compare the performance of different campaigns at a glance.
- This information can be used to optimize future Instagram marketing strategies.
