MESSAGE: Here's a pie chart visualizing the distribution of customer segments within Germany.  The chart shows the proportion of customers belonging to each segment. This allows for a quick understanding of the customer base composition in Germany.
SQL: ```sql
SELECT
  d.segment,
  COUNT(*) AS customer_count
FROM dim_customer AS d
JOIN dim_country AS c
  ON d.country_id = c.country_id
WHERE
  c.country_name = 'Germany'
GROUP BY
  d.segment;
```
